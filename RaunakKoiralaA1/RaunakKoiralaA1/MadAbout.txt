~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
       The Mad Libs Story Generator
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 "Mad Libs is a phrasal template word game 
 where one player prompts others for a list
 of words to substitute for blanks in any
 story, before reading the - often comical
 or nonsensical - story aloud. 

 The game is frequently played as a party 
 game or as a pastime." Wikipedia
